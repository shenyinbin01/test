# Chapter 003 Draft

The final checkpoint did not have a line.

It had a table, a clerk, and a board with three marks: clean pass, provisional mark, deferred attempt. The clean pass box was polished from use. The provisional box was almost untouched.

Mara's gray delay strip lay beside her pierced token and the witness receipt with Lio's name. Three pieces of evidence. None of them added up to a clean pass.

The clerk read the receipts. "You may claim clean pass if your witness confirms the white strip was unavailable."

Lio was close enough to hear. Renn was close enough too.

Mara could say yes and force Lio either to support her or contradict her in front of the clerk. She could let him take the blame for choosing gray. She could admit the truth: white had been available, but not clean for a marked witness.

Clean pass would move her up. Provisional mark would keep her inside the assessment and put the cost on record.

The clerk dipped the pen.

"I am not claiming clean pass," Mara said.

## Evidence: provisional-mark

The clerk paused. "Reason?"

"The white strip was available. It was not reliable for my witness."

Lio looked at her then. Not warmly. Not with forgiveness. But he looked.

The clerk wrote provisional advancement beside Mara's name. "You continue with restricted access. Witness debt remains active until reviewed."

Renn muttered something under her breath. It sounded like a laugh, but it ended too quickly.

## Evidence: ally-friction

Outside the checkpoint room, Lio took the witness receipt back from Mara and folded it into his own sleeve.

"You told the truth after the cost was already mine," he said.

Mara had an answer ready and did not use it.

The provisional mark kept her in the assessment. It also marked every future entry as restricted. She had not failed the three checkpoints. She had not passed cleanly either.

Lio walked beside her until the corridor forked. At the left turn, he stopped.

"Next time," he said, "ask before you decide what protects me."

Mara nodded. The words did not repair anything. They only kept the debt visible.

The assessment board changed her status before the third bell: provisional, restricted access, witness debt active. It was not victory. It was a state she could not pretend away.
