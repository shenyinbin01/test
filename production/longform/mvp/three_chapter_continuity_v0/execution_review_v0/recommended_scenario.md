# Recommended Scenario

## Recommendation

Choose Candidate C: `scenario_C_small_organization_advancement`.

## Reasoning

- Minimal world slice.
- No complete IP universe required.
- Natural volume goal.
- Three chapters can form visible continuity.
- Tests resource, relationship debt, knowledge state, reader question, and spotlight budget.
- Lower risk than a large-world conspiracy or complex hidden-truth setup.

## Guardrails

- Keep the organization abstract and local.
- Use one assessment rule only.
- Use one constrained resource only.
- Use one active relationship debt only.
- Do not create a real longform outline.
- Do not write scene prose during setup.

| chapter_002 | Force the prior delta to constrain choice and refresh relationship debt. |
