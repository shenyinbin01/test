# Human Review Rubric

Score each dimension 1-5.

| Dimension | What To Look For |
|---|---|
| reader_pull | Do you want the next chapter? |
| character_alive | Does the focal character feel like a person under pressure? |
| agency_depth | Does the choice arise from desire, fear, shame, misbelief, and cost? |
| scene_heat | Do tactics collide and change the situation? |
| subtext | Is something important withheld, misread, or negotiated under the words? |
| anti_ai_surface | Does the text avoid generic AI smoothness and process feel? |
| anti_explanation | Does it trust the reader to infer instead of explaining its own design? |
| narrative_stance | Does focal distance and detail selection feel controlled? |
| relationship_pressure | Does a relationship change through behavior or consequence? |
| prose_naturalness | Does the prose move without visible machinery or decorative overcompensation? |

Human preference outranks checklist score.
