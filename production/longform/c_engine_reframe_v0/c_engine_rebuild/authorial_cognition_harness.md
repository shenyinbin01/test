# Authorial Cognition Harness

## Definition

Authorial Cognition Harness is a private backend structure that carries author intent, character mind, scene pressure, and reader decompression. It exists to help the system think like a story-maker before rendering, not to put more engineering labels into prose.

The harness converts structural truth into a natural-language scene brief:

- What should the reader worry about?
- What does the focal character want but not admit?
- What shame, fear, misbelief, or self-image cost narrows the character's choices?
- What relation debt changes because of this scene?
- What does the reader know that the character does not?
- What should be left unsaid so the reader has room to infer?

## Why `agency_choice` Is Insufficient

`agency_choice` can prove that a character did something, but it cannot prove that the choice felt inevitable from inside that character.

A character is not alive because a field says “choice A over choice B.” A character becomes readable when the available choices are constrained by desire, fear, shame, misbelief, face cost, debt, social rules, and information gaps. The choice should feel like the only flawed tactic the character can bear to use at that moment.

## What Must Be Modeled

- desire: the local thing the character wants.
- fear: what they are trying not to lose.
- shame: what they cannot admit about the want.
- misbelief: the wrong model they are acting under.
- self-image cost: what the choice does to who they think they are.
- relationship debt: what they owe, avoid, exploit, or fail to repay.
- available tactic: what they could do.
- rejected tactic: what they refuse and why.
- social constraint: what the room, rank, institution, or relationship forbids.
- reader information gap: what the reader can infer before the character can.
- consequence felt by reader: what new worry remains after the scene.

## Why Raw Fields Must Not Feed Writer Directly

Raw fields encourage process prose. The Writer starts explaining the mechanism instead of making people act under pressure. Field names such as relationship debt, reader question, state_delta, or agency choice are backend terms. If they appear in prose or become sentence logic, the fiction reads like a report.

Therefore the harness compiles private packets into:

1. a natural-language scene brief,
2. a narrative stance brief,
3. a safe continuity summary,
4. optional rights-clear craft examples.

The Writer should see the pressure, not the machinery.
