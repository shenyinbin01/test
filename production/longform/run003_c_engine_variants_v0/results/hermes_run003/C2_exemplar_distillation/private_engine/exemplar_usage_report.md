## Chapter 001 — Exemplar Application

### character_pressure (primary)
**Principle applied**: Protagonist chooses flawed tactic because admitting cleaner option would expose shame/debt/fear.

**How applied**:
- Lin Yan receives an informal signal (Zhao Rui's coded message) and Su Tang's intel simultaneously.
- Cleaner option: share both signals with Su Tang, cross-validate, coordinate.
- Flawed tactic chosen: stay silent, pick the formal path (path 2) without consulting either source.
- The rejected tactic is made visible through his physical behavior: gripping phone until knuckles whiten, hesitation in the break room.
- Cost is exposed through behavior: Su Tang puts down her thermos with a loud clink, leaves without looking back.
- Agency is never stated. The reader infers: he stays silent because speaking would mean admitting he's afraid of owing her more — a callback to the department meeting incident.

### scene_heat
**Principle applied**: Each character wants something different, tactic fails, counter-tactic appears, irreversible mark left.

**How applied**:
- Objective: Su Tang wants confirmation that Lin Yan treats her as a partner (private want).
- Obstacle: Lin Yan's silence.
- Tactic (Su Tang): offer intel openly, create a moment for reciprocity ("which path are you taking?").
- Counter-tactic (Lin Yan): deflect, claim he'll "review the formal notice first."
- Turn: Zhao Rui texts Su Tang revealing Lin Yan chose path 2 — the counter-tactic is exposed.
- Irreversible mark: Su Tang's thermos clink, her single-word response "行", her walking away without looking back. The trust gap is seeded.

### reader_decompression
**Principle applied**: Leave inference room. Delay full answer when emotional cost > fact.

**How applied**:
- The chapter ends with Su棠 sitting alone in the cafeteria, noodles gone cold, staring at Zhao Rui's read-but-unanswered message.
- Reader sees: she's waiting for Lin Yan to reach out. She doesn't ask. She's learned not to.
- Reader question seeded: What would have happened if he'd shared? This question is left unanswered.
- The final line ("有些事情一旦学会了不问，就很难再学会开口") provides thematic closure without resolving the plot tension.
- Lin Yan does not know Su Tang is in the cafeteria. Reader knows more than protagonist.

## Chapter 002 — Exemplar Application

### character_pressure
**Principle applied**: Make rejected tactic and cost visible through behavior. Do not state agency.

**How applied**:
- Lin Yan's old tactic (silence, solo digestion) cannot be reused. The cost is now visible: Su Tang no longer puts her thermos down near him.
- When Zhao Rui reveals Lin Yan had the coded message, Lin Yan's physical reaction (back sweat going cold instantly, not being able to look Su Tang in the eye) shows the cost without stating it.
- Lin Yan's admission "I'm not saying I don't trust you" is undermined by his behavior — he came to explain but still can't say the real reason.
- Su Tang's line "You trust me but you're afraid to speak — how is that different from not trusting" articulates the reader's insight while Lin Yan still can't process it.

### scene_heat
**Principle applied**: scene_heat = objective + obstacle + tactic + counter-tactic + turn + cost.

**How applied**:
- Objective: Gate 02 requires coordination between Lin Yan and Su Tang.
- Obstacle: shattered trust from Gate 01.
- Tactic (Su Tang, self-protective): complete the entire collaboration framework alone, leave no decision spaces for Lin Yan. Her "不需要" (don't need anything) is both rejection and preemptive self-defense.
- Counter-tactic (Lin Yan, failed): attempt explanation, fail. Send "方案很好。需要我做什么" — a question that shows he still doesn't know how to reach her.
- Turn: Lin Yan discovers the scoring rubric penalizes imbalance. Su Tang's "不需要" isn't just rejection — it's her silently absorbing the imbalance penalty for him.
- Cost: Lin Yan realizes the tactic he used in Gate 01 cost more than his own score. It cost Su Tang's willingness to be vulnerable.

### reader_decompression
**Principle applied**: Reader sees one more consequence than focal character can admit.

**How applied**:
- Lin Yan discovers the scoring rubric's imbalance penalty and realizes Su Tang is protecting him — but he doesn't fully admit what this means about her feelings.
- The reader sees: Su Tang is still protecting him even while hurt. Her coldness is armor, not indifference.
- The extra comma she left in the framework document — a pause, a hesitation — is a detail Lin Yan notices but can't interpret. The reader can.
- Chapter ends with Lin Yan's realization that the harder question isn't whether you dare take someone's hand — it's whether you can find the way to speak again after it's been withdrawn.

## Chapter 003 — Exemplar Application

### character_pressure
**Principle applied**: Character chooses flawed tactic because admitting cleaner option would expose shame/debt/fear.

**How applied**:
- Cleaner option: choose option 1 (withdraw) or option 2 (demotion) — both keep the archive clean.
- Flawed tactic: choose option 3 — full promotion but requires Su Tang's signature confirming his deficiencies. This forces him to face her.
- The choice is structurally flawed: any option leaves a stain. But option 3 is the only one that forces him to "speak" — to tell her directly what he's doing and why.
- The note he leaves under her thermos ("你可以不签。我已经做好降级晋升的准备") shows he's not demanding her signature — he's finally communicating, even if imperfectly.
- The cost: 3-year archive record + elevated next-cycle threshold + another layer of debt to Su Tang.

### scene_heat
**Principle applied**: Each scene turn leaves irreversible mark.

**How applied**:
- Objective: pass Gate 03.
- Obstacle: the assessment requires visible cost; past behavior pattern identified as "collaborative trust deficiency."
- Tactic (Lin Yan): choose the option requiring Su Tang's signature and pre-notify her via note.
- Counter-tactic arrives indirectly: Su Tang signs AND leaves a note saying "三年档案换一条消息，不算贵" (three years of archive for one message — not expensive).
- Turn: the signature isn't given because of his note — it's given because he finally messaged her. The metric she's tracking isn't professional duty, it's whether he learned to speak.
- Irreversible mark: archive record locked for 3 years; relationship debt compounded; next evaluation threshold raised.

### reader_decompression
**Principle applied**: Leave incomplete reader question. Delay full answer.

**How applied**:
- The advancement is "non-clean" — he passes but with a permanent record.
- Su Tang's signing note reframes the cost: "不算贵" (not expensive). But this framing is her perspective, not necessarily the truth.
- Reader question left incomplete: Was this advancement worth the 3-year record? Will Lin Yan actually change his pattern, or will the next cycle repeat the same dynamic?
- Su Tang's final one-character reply "好" is deliberately ambiguous — acceptance? warning? hope? The reader must decide.
- The closing line ("不干净地往前走，比干净地站在原地更接近答案") provides thematic resolution without plot closure. The relationship debt = next friction is clearly seeded.

## Anti-Technique Verification
- No character stated their own agency or motivation in exposition.
- No scene described using engineering vocabulary.
- No "reader should think X" passages.
- All emotional states shown through physical behavior, environmental detail, and subtext.
- All three principles applied without naming them in prose.
