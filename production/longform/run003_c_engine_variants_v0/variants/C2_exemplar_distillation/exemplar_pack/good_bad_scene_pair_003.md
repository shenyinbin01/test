# Good/Bad Scene Pair 003

## Craft Axis

`reader_decompression`

## Bad Pattern

The prose explains what the reader should think, what the relationship means, and why the clue matters.

## Good Pattern

The prose leaves a gap: the reader sees one more consequence than the focal character can admit. The scene ends before all meaning is named.

## Transferable Principle

Leave inference room. Delay the full answer when the emotional cost is more important than the fact.

## Forbidden Surface Imitation

Do not imitate a specific author or reuse protected text.
