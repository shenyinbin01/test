# Good/Bad Scene Pair 001

## Craft Axis

`character_pressure`

## Bad Pattern

The character chooses the correct tactic because the plot needs progress. The scene explains that he has agency.

## Good Pattern

The character chooses a flawed tactic because admitting the cleaner option would expose shame, debt, or fear. The reader can infer why he cannot choose cleanly.

## Transferable Principle

Do not state agency. Make the rejected tactic and the cost of the chosen tactic visible through behavior.

## Forbidden Surface Imitation

Do not copy wording, cadence, setting objects, or character names from any outside work.
