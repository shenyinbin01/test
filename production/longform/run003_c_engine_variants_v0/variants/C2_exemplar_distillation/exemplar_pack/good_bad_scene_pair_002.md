# Good/Bad Scene Pair 002

## Craft Axis

`scene_heat`

## Bad Pattern

Characters exchange information politely until the chapter objective is satisfied.

## Good Pattern

Each character wants something different from the same exchange. A tactic fails, a counter-tactic appears, and the scene leaves an irreversible mark.

## Transferable Principle

Scene heat comes from objective, obstacle, tactic, counter-tactic, turn, and cost.

## Forbidden Surface Imitation

Do not add shouting, metaphor, or scenery as a substitute for pressure.
