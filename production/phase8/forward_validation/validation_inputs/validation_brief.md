# Validation Brief — Phase 8 Step 6 Sandbox

## Story Concept

**Title**: 十秒先知 (The Ten-Second Prophet)

**Genre**: 都市 / 科幻 / 悬疑 (Urban Sci-Fi Mystery)

**Core premise**: In a near-future cyberpunk city (Neo-Harbor, 2047), detective 林深 (Lin Shen) has a secret: he can see exactly 10 seconds into the future. He uses this for cases — knowing which door the suspect will run through, which shot to dodge. But today, during a routine investigation, he sees something in his 10-second window that he shouldn't: a figure in the vision looks directly at him and whispers "found you." His ability, he realizes, is not a gift — it's a leash. And someone is holding the other end.

## Validation Goals

This single-chapter validation tests whether Phase 8 approved patterns can:
1. Create a compelling protagonist engine (cognitive advantage + internalized cost)
2. Build reader pull through mystery hooks
3. Structure satisfying cognitive contrast moments
4. Support multi-dimensional review
5. Improve polish quality (de-AI, vividness)

## Constraints

- No xianxia elements
- No 《大乘期》characters or settings
- All worldbuilding must be original
- ~3000字 target
- Single chapter, self-contained with chapter-end pull
