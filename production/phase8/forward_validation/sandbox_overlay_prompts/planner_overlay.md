## Injected Patterns

### DCQG-C001: 认知碾压式爽点 (Cognitive Overwhelm)
**Core insight**: Entertainment comes not from hard-fought victory but from the protagonist's cognitive dimension far exceeding the current environment.
**Apply when**: Designing the protagonist's unique advantage in the story world.
**For this story**: The 10-second precognition IS the cognitive advantage. Design scenarios where this ability reveals hidden truths about the world that others can't see.

### DCQG-C009: 压力源内化 (Internalized Pressure)
**Core insight**: Beyond external enemies, the protagonist's internal pressure (responsibility, unknown costs, moral weight) sustains reader engagement for long arcs.
**Apply when**: Designing stakes that go beyond "defeat the villain."
**For this story**: The precognition's cost — each use drains life force, and discovering this IS the internal pressure.

### DCQG-C011: 多重介入身份 (Multi-Role Intervention)
**Core insight**: Protagonist operates through multiple identity layers (detective + reluctant hero + hidden knowledge bearer).
**Apply when**: Designing the protagonist's position in the world.
**For this story**: Detective by day, underground informant by night, and now — someone who knows too much.

### DCQG-C012: 世界观揭秘驱动 (Worldbuilding Revelation Driver)
**Core insight**: Each story phase reveals a layer of the world's hidden mechanics, keeping the mystery engine running.
**Apply when**: Structuring world information release.
**For this story**: Phase 1: precognition exists. Phase 2: it has a cost. Phase 3: the cost is designed by someone.

### DCQG-C010: 冲突螺旋升级 (Conflict Spiral)
**Core insight**: Conflicts escalate in a spiral — not linear — where each victory reveals a larger threat.
**Apply when**: Designing conflict arcs.
**For this story**: Small case → organized crime → government cover-up → magical conspiracy.

### DCQG-C014: 降维打击式引入 (Dimensional Reduction Intro)
**Core insight**: Introduce new world elements by contrasting them against what characters already "know" to be true.
**Apply when**: Timing world reveals.
**For this story**: The magical underground should be introduced as a contradiction to the cyberpunk surface world's logic.

### DCQG-C017: 多线并行织网 (Multi-Thread Weaving)
**Core insight**: Subplots should eventually converge, but in ways that recontextualize earlier events.
**Apply when**: Designing subplot structure.
**For this story**: Detective case + personal cost discovery + underground politics — weave together at the reveal point.
