## Injected Patterns

### DCQG-C001: 认知碾压式爽点 — Scene Version
**How to write**: Three beats per cognitive moment:
1. Side characters show their "obvious" understanding (1-2 lines of dialogue or description)
2. Protagonist shows a subtle reaction (a frown, a pause, a slight smile)
3. Protagonist reveals the truth in ONE sentence (≤30 characters)

### DCQG-C006: 伪装身份 (Identity Camouflage)
**How to write**: Show the tension between the protagonist's public identity and true knowledge. Don't narrate it — show it through:
- What they say vs. what they notice
- How others treat them vs. what they could do
- The moment the mask almost slips

### DCQG-C004: 外部任务触发器 (External Quest Trigger)
**How to write**: Open with a concrete external event that demands immediate action. Don't start with worldbuilding exposition. The quest IS the exposition vehicle.

### DCQG-C008: 历史沉浸揭秘 (Immersive History Revelation)
**How to write**: Don't info-dump backstory. Embed history in:
- Environmental details the protagonist notices
- A character's throwaway comment that means more than they know
- An object that carries a story

### DCQG-C003: 镜像反派 (Mirror Antagonist)
**How to write**: The antagonist should reflect a path the protagonist could have taken. Show:
- One moment where protagonist and antagonist make opposite choices in similar situations
- A shared vulnerability

### DCQG-C007: 主角作为导师 (Protagonist as Mentor)
**How to write**: Let the protagonist teach someone something — this reveals character depth more naturally than internal monologue.

### DCQG-C002: 规则解构式破局 (Rule Deconstruction Breakthrough)
**How to write**: The climax should NOT be "protagonist overwhelms with power." Instead:
1. Establish the "rules" everyone assumes to be true
2. Show the protagonist understanding a hidden implication
3. The protagonist uses the rule's own logic against the situation
