## Injected Patterns

### DCQG-C001: 认知碾压式爽点 — Polish Version
**Polish action**: Sharpen cognitive contrast moments:
- Make the protagonist's insight MORE concise (aim for ≤20 characters)
- Make the side characters' confusion MORE vivid (physical reactions, not just dialogue)
- Remove any narrative "explanation" between the confusion and the insight — let the reader feel the gap

### DCQG-C015: 理念冲突/价值观辩论 (Ideological Conflict)
**Polish action**: When characters disagree, make it about VALUES, not facts:
- Replace "No, the data shows..." with "You really believe that?"
- Add subtext: what a character argues FOR reveals what they're afraid of
- Remove false agreement — let disagreements stand unresolved

### DCQG-C021: 规则系统漏洞利用 (Rule System Exploit)
**Polish action**: When the protagonist uses cleverness, make the logic chain visible:
- Show the protagonist noticing the "edge case" before exploiting it
- Add one line where the protagonist tests a small assumption first
- Don't explain the logic — show the protagonist acting on it, let the reader connect dots

### DCQG-C022: 尾声初心回归 (Return to Origin)
**Polish action**: In the chapter's closing moment, echo something from the opening — a phrase, a gesture, a location. Create a subtle circular structure without announcing it.
