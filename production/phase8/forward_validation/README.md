## Key Findings

1. **Cognitive patterns (C001, C002) are the strongest** — they transfer cleanly across genres
2. **Internalized pressure (C009) works even better in non-xianxia** — the cost mechanism is universally applicable
3. **Reviewer hook pacing (C005) immediately improved audit quality** — systematic hook tracking
4. **C001 templating risk is real** — limit to 1 use per 2-3 chapters
5. **Arc-level patterns (C003, C007, C017)** need multi-chapter validation
